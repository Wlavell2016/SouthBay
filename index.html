About SouthBay Guesthouse

Spirit quest homes provides two relaxing locations for our guests to enjoy thier stays at. In the summer months our guests can relax on the shores of Georgian bay at our South Bay Guest house and in the winter months we offer accomdation at our house on Lake....Sudbury.

Our guest house is easily accessed via the Chi-Cheemaun. A 1.5 hour ferry from Tobermory
